Name: Adenium
Scientific Name: Desert Rose
Comman Name: Adenium
Discription: Adenium obesum is grown as a houseplant in temperate and tropical regions. Numerous hybrids have been developed. Adeniums are appreciated for their colorful flowers and unusual thick caudices. 
Uses: By pharmacological activities, these are considered to be antimicrobial, anticancer, antioxidant, antiviral. It also treats venereal diseases, muscle pain, joint pain, heart problems and many more.
Blomming Time: Early spring until mid-summer
Seasons: Early spring until mid-summer
