Name: Pansy
Scientific Name: viola and violet
Comman Name: Pansy
Discrip: The garden pansy is a type of large-flowered hybrid plant cultivated as a garden flower. It is derived by hybridization from several species in the section Melanium of the genus Viola, particularly V. tricolor, a wildflower of Europe and western Asia known as heartsease.
Uses: Flowering pansy herb is used externally to treat mild seborrheic skin diseases such as dandruff, itching, cradle cap and acne. Pansy is used for skin disorders that come from within. 
Blomming Time: spring to summer
Seasons:  fall to winter in hot areas.
