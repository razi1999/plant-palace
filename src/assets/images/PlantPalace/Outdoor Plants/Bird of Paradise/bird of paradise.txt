Name: Bird of Paradise
Sic Name: Strelitzia
Common Namw: Bird of Paradise
Discription:The birds-of-paradise are members of the family Paradisaeidae of the order Passeriformes. The majority of species are found in eastern Indonesia, Papua New Guinea and eastern Australia. The family has 45 species in 17 genera.
Uses: Uses. Bird-of-paradise are hardy, drought-tolerant plants that are easy to grow, especially in warm climates, and they are used extensively as ornamental landscape plants. They can also be grown as indoor houseplants in cold areas.
Blooming Times:  April to June.
Seasons: Late fall to late spring
