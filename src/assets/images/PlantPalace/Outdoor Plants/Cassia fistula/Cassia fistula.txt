Name: Cassia fistula
Scientific Name: Golden Shower Tree
Comman Name: Golden Shower Trees
Discription: Cassia is one of the most beautiful in all tropical trees when it sheds its leaves, and bursts into a mass of long, grape-bunches like yellow gold flowers.
Uses: Cassia fistula L possess hepatoprotective and antitussive characteristics. It contains antibacterial and antifungal properties. Cassia fistula L is used for healing of wounds and gastrointestinal illness. 
Blomming Time: late spring/early summer
Seasons: late spring/early summer
