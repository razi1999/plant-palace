Name: Areca Palm
Scientific Name: Areca Palm
Comman Name: golden cane palm
Discription: The Areca Palm, scientifically known as Dypsis lutescens or Chrysalidocarpus lutescens, is a popular tropical indoor and outdoor plant that belongs to the Arecaceae family. It is commonly grown as an ornamental plant for its attractive appearance and air-purifying qualities. 
Uses: In suitable climates, Areca Palms can be grown outdoors as well. They are often used in landscaping to create tropical-themed gardens or as accent plants around pools, patios, and walkways.
Blomming Time: late spring or early summer
Seasons: spring / early summer
